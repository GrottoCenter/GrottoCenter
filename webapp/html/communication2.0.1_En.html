GrottoCenter 2.0
New year, new GrottoCenter !!

GrottoCenter's new version shows up! It's fresh, it has an aroma of clay, it's still the same but it has gained in fluidity and now has many new features.

Before : GrottoCenter was opening directly with a map containing all entries, cavers and grottoes registered. Its problem was its loading time.

Now : you arrive on a home page whith a presentation and a synthesis ... From there, you go either to the map, either directly to the database. Since late January, more than 5300 caves are listed in over 26 different countries!

So see you soon on GrottoCenter.org !

What's new :
  • Easier to navigate by "clustering" (grouping) and filtering data.
  • Zoom tools and possibility to go back to the previous view.
  • The entry sheet contains more features: detailed history, bibliography, description of equipment...
  • My Account: avatar, personalized message, customizable browser settings...
  • Coordinates: management of different geodesic systems (including Lambert I, II, IIe, III and IV).
  • Possibility to link entries to partner web sites (post your ads!).
  • Improvement of ergonomics: who-is-connected list, top 10 contributors, list of the last registered, list of recent posts...
  • Very powerful and fast filtering tool: lists cavers, clubs and caves.
  • Don't get lost anymore thanks to the Forum, FAQs and user manual!
  • ...and many other features you will find browsing GrottoCenter!
